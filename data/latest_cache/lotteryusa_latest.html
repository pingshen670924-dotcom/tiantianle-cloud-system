Things to know

  1. The top prize starts between $60,000 and $80,000 and increases each draw until someone wins.
  2. If there are multiple top prize winners in the same draw, the top prize is split equally between them.
  3. The top three prizes of Fantasy 5 are pari-mutuel. This means a percentage of ticket sales is allocated to each prize category, so the prizes can vary every draw.
  4. Approximately 50% of the total Fantasy 5 ticket sales is allocated to the prize pool. 40% of that amount is allocated to the top prize.
  5. In Advance Play, you can enter your numbers in up to 12 consecutive draws.

FAQs
